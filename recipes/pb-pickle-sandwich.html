PEANUT BUTTER AND PICKLE SANDWICH

Peanut Butter and Pickle Sandwich recipe

Try this sandwich if you're craving something different.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 3 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2-3 pickles sliced lengthwise

Directions

  1. spread the peanut butter on both pieces of bread.
  2. place the pickles on one piece of bread.
  3. add the other piece of bread ontop to finish the sandwich.
