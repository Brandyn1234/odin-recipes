Sesame Garlic Ramen Noodles recipe

Easy recipe to add some flavor to ramen noodles

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of ramen noodles
  • 2 teaspoons of sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons sriracha

Directions

  1. cook the ramen noodles without the seasoning. Drain the water once finished cooking.
  2. heat sesame oil in skillet on medium heat.
  3. cook the garlic in the sesame oil.
  4. remove pan from heat and mix in the soy sauce, brown sugar, and sriracha until combined.
  5. toss the noodles with the sauce.
